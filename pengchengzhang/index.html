I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics from Jan 2022 and my supervisor is Don Zagier. Before coming to Bonn, I was a MMath student at University of Oxford and my master supervisor was Alan Lauder. Here is a copy of my CV.
I am generally interested in number theory, especially (arithmetic properties of) \(L\)-functions and automorphic forms. My current research projects are related to
  • arithmetic properties of Hecke eigenvalues of modular forms (see the paper by Buzzard);
  • converse theorems for (Hilbert) modular forms (see the paper by Conrey and Farmer);
  • magnetic modular forms (see the paper by Pa≈üol and Zudilin).
